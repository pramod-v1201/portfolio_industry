About

Dr. Pramod Vishwakarma is an accomplished academic and researcher with over 16 years of professional experience spanning academia, EduTech, and technical training. Throughout his career, Dr. Vishwakarma has been actively involved in the design and delivery of specialized training programs for a diverse audience, including corporate professionals, university students, and government employees—most notably with the State Bank of India (SBI).

He has authored and published 25 research papers and one book chapter in reputed national and international journals and conferences, including those by IEEE and Springer. Additionally, he holds one patent and has authored two books in the field of Data Science.His research and teaching interests includes Data Science, Deep Learning, Cybersecurity, and Image Processing, with a strong focus on MATLAB and Python-based implementations..
